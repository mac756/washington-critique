Benjamin Franklin

1706-1790

Diplomat & Statesman

Secured French alliance that saved the revolution. Scientist, writer, master negotiator.

Wikimedia Commons (Public Domain)

Marquis de Lafayette

1757-1834

French General

Young French noble who joined at 19. Became Washington's protégé and surrogate son.

Rembrandt Peale / Wikimedia Commons (Public Domain)

Thomas Jefferson

1743-1826

Author of Independence

Wrote the Declaration. Governor of Virginia during British invasion — fled Richmond.

Wikimedia Commons (Public Domain)

Benedict Arnold

1741-1801

Traitor (Former Hero)

Brilliant tactician at Saratoga. Betrayed West Point. Name became synonymous with treason.

Allan Ramsay / Wikimedia Commons (Public Domain)

King George III

1738-1820

British Monarch

Refused to see colonies as equals. Lost America due to arrogance and inflexibility.
